Our Projects

A showcase of our recent work and partnerships.

Maxim

We collaborated with Maxim to develop and produce high-quality custom stickers, banners, and other printed materials. From concept to print, we worked closely to ensure vibrant designs and durable finishes that effectively showcase their brand.

PahangGO

We partnered with PahangGO to design and produce eye-catching banners that highlight their services and initiatives. The banners were crafted for maximum visibility and impact, ensuring strong brand presence no matter the place & time.

KTM

We worked with KTM to install full train wraps and backseat advertisements, transforming their trains into dynamic moving billboards. This high-impact campaign boosted brand visibility and engaged commuters across key routes.

Sunway Lagoon

We collaborated with Sunway Lagoon to create vibrant banners and stickers that enhance the park's attractions and promotional efforts. Designed for durability and visual appeal, these materials added a lively and engaging touch throughout the venue.

Regent International School

We partnered with Regent International School to design and produce custom shirts and banners for their events. The cohesive designs reflected the school’s identity, adding a professional and spirited touch to their celebrations and activities.

Intel
